About Me

Being born and raised in the heart of it all, the great state of Ohio, many might expect that I am a die hard OSU fan and the assumption is correct. I like to think of myself as a secret supporter due to the burden of keeping up the fanatic guise. Frankly, I only care about football when I am amongst my fellow buckeyes. I suspect that there are others out there. Some who feel that Brutus does not quite get how we feel. We are the forgotten ones. We bleed scarlet and gray from dawn to the dying of the light. We are the ultimate spectators. No need for loudness. No need for ruidness. We simply exist, we live life, we watch football, we get excited on occasion. We know that no matter what we are "Buckeyes". Wins and losses won't change my mood. It won't make me depressed. No chaos required.

Enter, Aspiring Web Developer Stage left. Yes, I know I have just refered to my self in the third person, but that is how it is. But let us, let the main thing be the main thing. My method of solving problems is to visualize the solution as a bag of cats waiting to be released. Wierd analogy, right? We have to release the cats and let all the chaos out, before the underlying issue can be solved. You could say that I am comfortable with a certain level of it. I have found that once all the questions have had their day in the sun, the solution is obvious. In short, my name is Kourtney, I like to find solutions in the chaos, while contributing to it as little as possible. I am usually successful at both.
